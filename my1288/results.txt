This file gives a description of the income per person distribution for the years 2007 to 2012. 

Looking at the Income per person distribution for the years 2007 to 2012 it can be observed that the overall distribution has not changed that much. A peak is seen between the income range of 0 to 5000 in all the years. All of the distributions have a notable gap in the range 50000 to 70000.
